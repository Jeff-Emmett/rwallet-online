How It Works

From Address to Insight

1

Enter Address

Paste any Safe multi-sig wallet address. rWallet checks all supported chains in parallel.

2

Fetch Live Data

Transactions, balances, and transfers are pulled directly from the Safe Global API — no backend needed.

3

Visualize

Choose from three interactive visualization modes to understand fund flows, balances over time, and cross-chain activity.

4

Share & Verify

Every view has a shareable deep-link. Anyone can verify the data independently — full transparency.

Three Ways to Explore

Each visualization reveals different aspects of your wallet's activity.

🌊

Balance River Timeline

Watch funds flow through the wallet over time. The river thickness represents balance, with inflows and outflows color-coded for instant comprehension.

  • Scroll to zoom into time periods
  • Drag or shift-scroll to pan through history
  • Hover the river for balance at any point
  • Flow width proportional to transaction size
🔗

Multi-Chain Flow Analysis

See fund flows across all detected chains in one view. Filter by chain to drill into Gnosis, Ethereum, Base, or any other active network.

  • Auto-detects chains with Safe deployments
  • Interactive chain filtering
  • Flow diagram with address-level detail
  • Stats breakdown by direction
📊

Single-Chain Sankey

Classic Sankey diagram showing the complete flow of funds on a single chain. Nodes represent addresses, links represent value transferred.

  • Per-chain Sankey flow diagram
  • Address-level fund flow breakdown
  • Chain selector for multi-chain wallets
  • Transaction details on hover
